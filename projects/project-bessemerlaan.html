Bessemerlaan 43 — Omgevingsvergunning

Year 2025
Role Architectural Drafter
Location Utrecht, NL
Tools AutoCAD, NEN 2580

Full permit documentation package for a residential renovation and cadastral split in Utrecht.

This project involved preparing a complete omgevingsvergunning application for a residential renovation at Bessemerlaan 43, including cadastral splitting documentation for the gemeente Utrecht.

Scope

Deliverables included projectomschrijving and fotobijlage documents following the office template, NEN 2580 area and volume calculations (BVO, GO, bruto inhoud), and ventilation and daylight compliance tables under NEN 1087 and NEN 2057.

Process

Work was coordinated through omgevingsloket.nl, accounting for Wkb (Wet kwaliteitsborging voor het bouwen) requirements and gemeente Utrecht-specific submission standards — bridging technical drafting with the procedural side of Dutch permit applications.
