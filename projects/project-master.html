Master's Research — Academie van Bouwkunst

Year 2023–present
Role Master's Candidate
Location Amsterdam, NL
Tools Revit, hand drawing, research

Ongoing Master's research at the Academie van Bouwkunst Amsterdam, focused on sustainable and earth-based construction.

My Master's work at AHK sits at the intersection of material research, structural reasoning, and renovation strategy — using rammed earth and hempcrete as test cases for how biobased materials can be integrated into existing Dutch building stock rather than only new construction.

Focus

The research draws on seven years of architectural training in Kharkiv, Ukraine, combined with hands-on technical practice gained at Bouwadvies Mur in Utrecht. This dual grounding — academic and professional — shapes a design approach that treats construction detail and material circularity as design drivers, not afterthoughts.

Ongoing

This is a living body of work, developed across multiple studio briefs and jury presentations, continuing toward a final thesis project.
