Civic & Community Visual Work

Year 2023–24
Role Graphic Design
Location Utrecht, NL
Tools Adobe Suite, Figma

Graphic design and visual identity work for humanitarian and civic initiatives in Utrecht.

Alongside architectural practice, I contribute graphic design work to community and humanitarian organisations — applying the same attention to layout, hierarchy, and material honesty used in architectural drawing to civic communication.

Work included

Visual identity and printed collateral for Vitalnya, a Ukrainian humanitarian organisation, supporting outreach and fundraising materials. Co-organisation and visual design for Lichtmars 2024 in Utrecht, a civic light event bringing communities together through public space.

Recognition

This community involvement was recognised through the Social Hub Foundation's Changemaker Scholarship Programme, supporting continued volunteer work alongside academic and professional commitments.
