About

El Mehdi Banacer
Architectural Designer
Utrecht, NL

An emerging architectural designer currently pursuing a Master's at the Academie van Bouwkunst in Amsterdam. My multicultural perspective has been shaped by Morocco, seven formative years in Ukraine where I completed my Bachelor of Architecture, and now the Netherlands.

I bring professional experience from INBO, HFB Architectuur, and Dutch Architect, where I specialised in BIM modelling, technical drawings, and design development for housing and mixed-use projects — from concept through to execution drawings.

Beyond commercial work, I apply my visual design skills to social causes, including materials for the Ukrainian organisation Vitalnya. My ambition is to integrate structural clarity, narrative depth, and social responsibility into every project.
