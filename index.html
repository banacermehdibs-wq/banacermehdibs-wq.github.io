About

El Mehdi
Architectural Designer
Utrecht, NL

Seven years of architecture in Kharkiv, Ukraine. Relocated to the Netherlands in 2022. Practice moves between hand drawing, computational tools, and material research.

At Bouwadvies Mur I prepare omgevingsvergunning applications, NEN 2580 calculations, and technical documentation for residential renovation. At the Academie van Bouwkunst I am completing a Master's focused on sustainable and earth-based construction.

Social Changemaker, Social Hub Foundation. Graphic design for Vitalnya Ukrainian humanitarian organisation. Co-organiser, Lichtmars 2024, Utrecht.
